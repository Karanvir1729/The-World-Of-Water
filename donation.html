UNICEF

UNICEF supports a wide variety of causes ranging from famine to water accessibilty. UNICEF has worked with 100 countries worldwide to provide clean drinking water aswell as proper water sanitisation transforming the live of many children around the world. They have provided 14 million people with fresh water and 11 million with toilets.

For more information visit UNICEF

Pure Water For The World

Pure water for the world aims to provide clean water by parternering with developing countries with prominent water hygiene issues. They not only support these places but provide education pertaining to water diseases and hygiene surrounding them, creating sustainable communities.

Water For Good

Water for Good focuses on the issue of water accessibility. Many children have to walk miles before they can get access to water Water For Good solves this issue by building wells for these communities. They have drilled over 1000 wells in central Africa and are continuing to build more.

For more information visit Water For Good

Miya

Miya is a water charity that has value-based and efficiency-based approaches to solving the water crisis. They work with local communities by delivering educational programs and projects on water sustainability. They ensure the efficiency of urban-water systems to provide the best resource management.

For more information visit Miya
